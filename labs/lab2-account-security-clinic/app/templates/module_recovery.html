Resilience checklist

Minimum viable: A known recovery email/phone, device lock enabled, and a documented reset process (who verifies identity, how fast, and what evidence is required).
Better: Password manager adoption + recovery codes stored inside it, plus documented steps for lost phones and staff turnover.
Common failure mode: MFA is enabled but no one knows the recovery plan. The first lost phone becomes a major incident.
Discussion activity: - Define who can initiate a reset - Define how identity is verified - Define how long it should take - Decide what gets logged (audit trail)
